About Our Weapons

Dedicated professionals guiding you through the art of stage combat with these weapons in Austin June 8th and 9th 2024.

Meet Our Weapons

Single Sword

From the quick and light Errol Flynn Hollywood Single Sword to the one handed saber and even the grosse messer, Capital Combat students can take classes in swords wielded in one hand. Through practical exercises and expert instruction, actors will refine their technique and choreography to deliver captivating sword fights on stage.

Knife

The Knife is the scariest stage combat weapon because the audience understands it so well. Take classes focusing on stage combat with knives, developing the art of choreographing realistic and safe fight scenes to enhance theatrical productions. Participants will develop the skills necessary to portray convincing and dynamic knife fights on stage.

Rapier & Dagger

Are you ready to join the Musketeers? Capital Combat Students can take classes centered on rapier and dagger combat, honing their skills in the art of dual-wielding blades for compelling and intricate stage fights.

Small Sword

Popularized in the 18th century French dueling culture, the smallsword is a deadly thrusting weapon. Sip cognac in your off-hand while upholding your honor … to the death!

Unarmed

Unarmed is on some ways the best way to begin learning stage combat. From slaps to punches to kicks to chokes to hip tosses and hair pulls, there are myriad ways to tell beautiful and dangerous looking stories with only our bodies.

Quarter Staff

What is the best thing about the quarter staff? It’s cheap! And packs a whollup! Join Robin Hood and the Merry Band and learn how to beat people up (at least pretend to) with a 7 foot long stick!

Sword & Shield

Let’s get medieval! Sword and Shield was the weapon system preferred by the medieval knight. And what an incredible sound actors can make with Sword & Shield. Capital Combat students can take beginner and advanced classes in S&S.

Longsword

Often referred to as Broadsword in SAFD terminology, the longsword is a two-handed weapon of art, grace, and deadly form. Many historical fight manuals that focus on the longsword exist to this day. Join us at Capital Combat and you can flip through them!
