About Our Weapons

Dedicated professionals guiding you through the art of stage combat with these weapons in Austin June 8th and 9th 2024.

Meet Our Weapons

Single Sword

Participants will immerse themselves in a dynamic workshop focusing on single sword combat, mastering the intricacies of wielding a blade for theatrical performances with authenticity and precision. Through practical exercises and expert instruction, actors will refine their technique and choreography to deliver captivating sword fights on stage.

Knife

Actors will engage in a comprehensive workshop focusing on stage combat with knives, mastering the art of choreographing realistic and safe fight scenes to enhance theatrical productions. Through hands-on training and expert guidance, participants will develop the skills necessary to portray convincing and dynamic knife fights on stage.

Rapier & Dagger

Actors will delve into a specialized workshop centered on rapier and dagger combat, honing their skills in the art of dual-wielding blades for compelling and intricate stage fights. With expert guidance and hands-on practice, participants will refine their coordination and timing to deliver thrilling and authentic duels in theatrical performances.

Small Sword

Participants will engage in a specialized workshop focusing on small sword combat, refining their technique and timing to deliver captivating and authentic stage duels under expert guidance and through hands-on practice.

Unarmed

Participants will undergo an intensive workshop focusing on unarmed combat techniques, mastering the art of stage fighting without weapons to deliver convincing and dynamic performances under expert guidance and through hands-on practice.

Quarter Staff

Participants will immerse themselves in a specialized workshop centered on quarterstaff combat, mastering the techniques of wielding the staff for theatrical performances with authenticity and precision under expert guidance and through hands-on practice.

Sword & Shield

Participants will engage in an intensive workshop focusing on sword and shield combat, mastering the art of dual-wielding weapons for compelling and dynamic stage fights under expert guidance and through hands-on practice.
